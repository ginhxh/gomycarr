What do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car, you'll typically need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and sometimes proof of insurance.

How old do I need to be to rent a car?

Most rental companies require drivers to be at least 21 years old, though some may charge a young driver surcharge for those under 25.

Can I rent a car without a credit card?

Some rental agencies may accept debit cards, but they often have additional requirements, such as a credit check or proof of travel plans.

Do I need insurance to rent a car?

While you’re not legally required to have insurance to rent a car, it’s advisable to either have your own insurance or purchase coverage offered by the rental company.
